Our Story

Built on hard work and dedication

Founded in 2020, Texas Tough Rentals was born from a simple idea: providing reliable, high-quality dump trailer rentals to homeowners and contractors throughout the Keller area. What started as a single trailer has grown into a thriving business serving the community with pride.

Our founder, Bob, recognized the need for affordable, dependable hauling solutions after years of working in construction and landscaping. He knew firsthand the frustration of unreliable equipment and poor customer service. That's why Texas Tough Rentals was built on three core principles: quality equipment, transparent pricing, and exceptional customer service.

Today, we're proud to serve residential and commercial clients throughout the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex, helping them tackle projects big and small with our fleet of well-maintained dump trailers.
